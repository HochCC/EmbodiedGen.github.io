EmbodiedGen Towards a Generative 3D World Engine for Embodied Intelligence

Xinjie Wang1, Liu Liu1, Yu Cao2, Ruiqi Wu5,1, Wenkang Qin2,

Dehui Wang4,3, Wei Sui3, Zhizhong Su1
1Horizon Robotics, 2GigaAI, 3D-Robotics, 4Shanghai Jiao Tong University, 5VCIP, CS, Nankai University
arXiv Video Code

EmbodiedGen enables highly controllable generation of rigid and articulated assets with accurate real-world scale and physical properties, along with stylistically background generation and visually rich texture editing. The 3D assets can be composed as interactive 3D worlds and seamlessly integrated into various simulators, leveraging generative AI to address the challenges of generalization in embodied intelligence.

Framework

EmbodiedGen is an easy-to-use platform composed of six key modules: Image-to-3D, Text-to-3D, Texture Generation, Articulated Object Generation, Scene Generation, Layout Generation to enable interactive 3D world generation with accurate physical properties and real-world scale at low cost.
